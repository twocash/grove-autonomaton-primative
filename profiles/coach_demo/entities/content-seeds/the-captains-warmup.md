# The Captain's Warm-Up

Pillar: chatard_stories
Theme: leadership

Jake didn't ask to run warm-ups. He just started showing up ten minutes early and stretching where the other guys could see him. After a week, they started joining. After two weeks, it was the routine. That's how leadership actually works — not a speech, not a title. Just a guy who shows up first and does the right thing until it becomes contagious. I didn't assign him captain because of his scoring average. I assigned him captain because the team was already following him before I said a word.
