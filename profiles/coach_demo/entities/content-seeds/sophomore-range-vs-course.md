# The Sophomore Who Couldn't Miss on the Range

Pillar: failure_redemption
Theme: resilience

One of our sophomores can stripe a 7-iron on the range like he was born doing it. Pure contact, dead straight, the sound you dream about. Then we walk to the first tee for a practice round and it's like someone switched the clubs. Topped drives. Fat chips. The yips, but not the kind you read about — the kind that comes from caring too much about what happens next. After nine holes last week, he looked at me and said, "Coach, I don't know why I can't do it out here." I told him: "You can. You just haven't learned to trust yourself when it counts yet. That's what this season is for." Grace isn't just for after the mistake. Grace is for the kid who's trying so hard it's getting in his own way.
