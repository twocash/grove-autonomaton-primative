# Eagle Creek at Dawn

Pillar: course_beauty
Theme: creation

There's a moment at Eagle Creek around 6:45 AM when the mist is still sitting in the fairways and the sun is just catching the tops of the oaks behind the 7th green. No one is out there yet. The sprinklers have stopped. You can hear everything — a cardinal, the creek, your own breathing. I stood on the 7th tee last Tuesday and thought: this is a cathedral with no roof. The heavens declare the glory of God, and the golf course declares it too — if you're quiet enough to listen.
