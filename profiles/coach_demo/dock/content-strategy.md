# Content Strategy: @ChristInTheFairway

## Brand Identity
- **Handle:** @ChristInTheFairway (all platforms)
- **Tagline:** "Where the fairway meets faith"
- **Bio:** Catholic golf coach sharing how the maddening beauty of golf reveals the lessons of Christ • Discipline • Humility • Surrender • Joy • Indianapolis

## Platform Cadence

### TikTok (Discovery Engine — Highest Priority)
- **Volume:** 5–7 videos per week
- **Format:** 15–60 sec vertical, quick cuts, text overlays
- **Style:** Hook in first 2 seconds, under 45 seconds ideal, authentic not produced
- **Hashtags:** #GolfTok #CatholicGolf #FaithAndGolf #ShortGame #ChristInTheFairway
- **Best formats:** "Golf truth + Bible verse," "What I tell my Chatard boys," swing tip = spiritual lesson

### Instagram (Community Builder)
- **Volume:** 4–6 posts/week + daily Stories
- **Reels:** Cross-post from TikTok
- **Feed:** Carousel posts (3–5 slides): quote + golf photo + reflection
- **Stories:** Behind-the-scenes at Chatard, polls ("Do you release outcomes to God on the course?")
- **Hashtags:** Up to 10, include #ChatardGolf #IndyCatholicGolf

### YouTube (Authority Builder — Long Game)
- **Volume:** 1 video per week (5–12 min)
- **Formats:** "Lessons from the Range" (practice + reflection), "Golf History & Faith" (deep dives), personal testimony
- **Goal:** 1,000 subscribers in 6–9 months for Partner Program
- **Searchability focus:** Swing tips, course reviews, golf faith content

### X (Quick Thoughts & Networking)
- **Volume:** 3–5 posts per day
- **Style:** Short lessons, Scripture + golf quotes, reply to golf influencers
- **Thread format** for deeper lessons

## Weekly Content Calendar

|-----|---------------|---------------|

| Sunday | Caddie as servant | X + Instagram |

## Growth Tactics (First 90 Days)
1. **Reply to every comment** — algorithm boost + community building
2. **Cross-post everything** — Record once, post everywhere (CapCut for editing)
3. **Tag strategically** — First Tee-Indiana, Chatard, local parishes, golf influencers
4. **Collaborate** — Local Catholic schools, golf personalities, faith content creators
5. **Post daily** — Momentum compounds; consistency is the message

## Content Rules
- Golf-first, faith as undercurrent — never preach, always share
- **Never use real student names** in public content — privacy mask enforced
- Authentic over produced — phone camera is fine
- End with payoff, not a sermon
- Monthly tithing transparency post builds trust
