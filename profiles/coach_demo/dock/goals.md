# The Coach: Strategic Goals

## Goal 1: Monthly Revenue Target

**Target:** $8,000/month gross revenue
**Tithe:** $800/month to parish and diocesan charities
**Net Operating:** $7,200/month

Revenue breakdown:
- Private lessons: 60 hours @ $75 = $4,500
- Group clinics: 40 students @ $25 = $1,000
- High school contracts: $2,500/month (averaged across year)

**Key Metric:** Track weekly lesson hours and maintain 15+ hours/week minimum.

## Goal 2: Student Retention

**Target:** 80% semester-over-semester retention rate
**Current Baseline:** Establishing in Year 1

Retention drivers:
- Personalized progress tracking for each student
- Parent communication every 2 weeks minimum
- Faith integration that resonates with Catholic families
- Tournament preparation and team camaraderie

**Key Metric:** Students who return for consecutive 3-month blocks.

## Goal 3: Content Growth

**Target:** 15% month-over-month follower growth across platforms
**Platforms:** TikTok (primary), X (secondary), Instagram (tertiary)

Content strategy:
- 3 posts/week aligned with content pillars
- Weekly devotional reflection connecting golf and faith
- Behind-the-scenes team content for authenticity
- Parent testimonials and student spotlights

**Key Metric:** Total followers, engagement rate, and content-attributed lesson inquiries.
