# @ChristInTheFairway: Strategic Goals

## The Mission

Every view, every subscriber, every dollar of revenue exists to fund the tithing mandate. These goals are milestones toward that mission.

---

## Goal 1: YouTube Subscribers

**Target:** 1,000 subscribers in 6 months
**Why:** YouTube Partner Program eligibility requires 1,000 subs + 4,000 watch hours

### Milestones
- Month 1: 100 subscribers (launch momentum)
- Month 2: 250 subscribers
- Month 3: 400 subscribers
- Month 4: 600 subscribers
- Month 5: 800 subscribers
- Month 6: 1,000 subscribers (Partner Program application)

### Actions
- 1 video per week minimum
- Focus on searchable content (swing tips, course reviews)
- Cross-promote from TikTok and Instagram
- Collaborate with local golf personalities

---

## Goal 2: TikTok Volume

**Target:** 5 TikToks per week (minimum)
**Why:** Algorithm rewards consistency; short-form is the discovery engine

### Weekly Breakdown
- Monday: Short-game devotional
- Tuesday: Chatard practice clip
- Wednesday: Swing thought/spiritual parallel
- Thursday: Failure & redemption story
- Friday: Course beauty or Indy Catholic golf

### Quality Standards
- Hook in first 2 seconds
- Under 45 seconds ideal
- Authentic, not produced
- Faith as undercurrent, not headline

---

## Goal 3: Initial Tithing Target

**Target:** $500/month tithing (20% of $2,500 gross revenue)
**Timeline:** Achieve within 6 months of launch

### Revenue Path to $500 Tithe
- $500 tithe = $2,500 gross revenue
- YouTube ads: ~$200/month (after monetization)
- Affiliate links: ~$100/month
- Lesson referrals: ~$200/month
- Speaking/events: ~$500/month (variable)
- Merch: Future phase

### Beneficiary Split (First $500)
- First Tee - Indiana: $167
- Chatard Golf Program: $167
- St. Vincent de Paul: $166

---

## Goal 4: Content Consistency

**Target:** Never miss a week
**Why:** Discipline is the message. If we can't be consistent, we can't preach discipline.

### Non-Negotiables
- [ ] 5 TikToks every week
- [ ] 3 Instagram posts every week
- [ ] 1 YouTube video every week
- [ ] Weekly review of analytics

---

## Goal 5: Community Engagement

**Target:** Respond to every comment within 24 hours
**Why:** We're building relationships, not an audience

### Engagement Rules
- Every comment gets a reply
- DMs answered same day
- Shoutouts to engaged followers
- Highlight community content

---

## Accountability

The Cortex (Lens 3) monitors telemetry and provides Kaizen proposals tied to these goals. When content is generated, the system reminds us:

*"Coach, 3 great videos this week. We are building the audience for the First Tee donation. Keep it up."*

Progress is tracked. Encouragement is automated. But the work is ours.
