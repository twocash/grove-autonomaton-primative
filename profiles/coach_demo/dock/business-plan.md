# Lessons of Christ in Golf: Business Plan

## Brand Identity

**Handle:** @ChristInTheFairway
**Tagline:** "Where the fairway meets faith"

## Mission

Build multi-platform content channels that teach golf through the lens of Catholic spirituality, generating revenue that directly supports youth golf and those in need in the Indianapolis community.

This is not a business. This is a ministry with a business model.

## The Vision

The game of golf teaches what classrooms cannot: patience, integrity, humility, and grace under pressure. Every swing is a prayer. Every round is a pilgrimage. We capture these moments and share them to inspire others—and the revenue we generate goes back to form the next generation.

## Revenue Streams

### Primary Channels
1. **YouTube** - Long-form content: swing breakdowns with spiritual parallels, course vlogs, player journey documentaries
2. **TikTok** - Short-form hooks: quick tips, faith moments, behind-the-scenes
3. **Instagram** - Visual storytelling: course beauty, team photos, reels

### Monetization
- YouTube Partner Program (ads)
- Affiliate partnerships (golf equipment, books)
- Private lesson referrals
- Speaking engagements at Catholic schools and parishes
- Merchandise (future)

## The Tithing Mandate

**We tithe 20%+ of all gross revenue.**

This is non-negotiable. The content exists to generate funds for:

### Primary Beneficiaries
1. **First Tee - Indiana** - Teaching life skills through golf to underserved youth
2. **Bishop Chatard High School Golf Program** - Equipment, course fees, scholarships
3. **St. Vincent de Paul Society** - Serving the poor in our community

### Distribution
- First $500/month: Split equally among all three
- Beyond $500: Weighted toward greatest need, decided quarterly

## Content Strategy

### Weekly Volume
- TikTok: 5 posts minimum
- Instagram: 3 posts minimum
- YouTube: 1 video per week (goal)

### Content Pillars
1. Short-game devotionals
2. Real-life Chatard stories (privacy-protected)
3. Golf history & virtue
4. Swing thoughts/spiritual parallels
5. Course beauty
6. Caddie as servant leadership
7. Failure & redemption
8. Local Indy Catholic golf community

### Voice
Warm, relatable, non-preachy. Golf-first with faith as the natural undercurrent. We don't lecture—we share. We don't perform faith—we live it on camera.

## Privacy Commitment

Because we work with minors, **no student names are ever used in public content**. All players are referenced by anonymous descriptors ("a freshman on the team," "one of our juniors"). This is enforced at the system level through the Privacy Mask.

## Success Metrics

This is a long game. We measure:
- Lives touched, not just views
- Consistent content creation (discipline)
- Monthly tithing amount (stewardship)
- Subscriber growth (reach)
- Community engagement (relationship)

The goal is not fame. The goal is faithfulness. The revenue is a tool for mission.
