# The Coach: Catholic Golf Instruction Business Plan

## Mission & Philosophy

The Coach exists to form young men of character through the discipline of golf. Rooted in the Catholic tradition, we believe that the golf swing is a metaphor for the spiritual life: it requires surrender, trust, repetition, and humility. Our coaching philosophy integrates the mechanical fundamentals of a sound swing with the theological virtues of faith, hope, and charity. Every lesson is an opportunity to build not just better golfers, but better men who will serve their families, parishes, and communities.

## Business Model & Revenue

Our primary revenue streams are private lessons ($75/hour), group clinics ($25/student), and seasonal team coaching contracts with Catholic high schools in the diocese ($2,500/season). We tithe 10% of all gross revenue to the parish and diocesan charitable funds, believing that our business is blessed when we give first. Secondary revenue includes tournament entry fees, equipment partnerships, and content monetization through social media platforms where we share faith-integrated golf instruction.

## Growth Strategy & Stewardship

Growth is measured not merely in dollars but in souls touched. We aim to maintain a student retention rate above 80% by building genuine relationships with players and their families. Content pillars—Surrender to the Swing, The Spiritual Swing, and Team as Communion—guide our social media presence, targeting 15% monthly follower growth. All business decisions are filtered through the question: "Does this honor God and serve the young men entrusted to our care?" Financial success follows faithfulness; we trust in Providence while working with excellence.
