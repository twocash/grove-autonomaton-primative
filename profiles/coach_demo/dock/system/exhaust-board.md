# Exhaust Board

> *Registry of Telemetry Signals and Future Automation Potentials*

This file is maintained by the Architectural Judge. When a new skill passes compliance validation, the Judge analyzes its telemetry exhaust and records potential future uses.

---

## Purpose

The Exhaust Board serves as a strategic registry for the Cortex (async analyzer) to consult when:
- Identifying automation opportunities
- Planning skill chains and compositions
- Discovering emergent patterns from telemetry

---

## Registry Format

Each entry follows this structure:

### [Skill Name] - [Date]

**Telemetry Unlocks:**
- Unlock 1: Description of how this skill's output can be consumed
- Unlock 2: Potential chain composition with other skills
- Unlock 3: Analytics or monitoring opportunity
