# Revenue & Tithing Plan

## The Mandate
We tithe 20%+ of all gross revenue. This is non-negotiable. The content exists to generate funds for the community.

## Revenue Streams

### Active Now
1. **Private Lesson Referrals** — $80–120/session, promoted in bio/link tree
2. **Affiliate Links** — Amazon (golf books, training aids, rangefinders, putting mats)

### After YouTube Monetization (1,000 subs + 4,000 watch hours)
3. **YouTube Partner Program** — Ads on long-form, ~$2–8 per 1,000 views
4. **Patreon / Buy Me a Coffee** — $5–10/month tiers: exclusive reflections, prayer for supporters, BTS Chatard clips. Target: 50–100 supporters = $250–1,000/month

### Future Phase
5. **Speaking Engagements** — Catholic schools, parishes, golf events. $200–500/event
6. **Merchandise** — After 5k–10k followers. Simple designs: "Release the Outcome" + Matthew 6:34

## Tithing Beneficiaries

### Primary (First $500/month split equally)
- **First Tee - Indiana** — Teaching life skills through golf to underserved youth
- **Bishop Chatard Golf Program** — Equipment, course fees, scholarships
- **St. Vincent de Paul Society** — Serving the poor in Indianapolis

### Growth Phase (Beyond $500/month)
- Weighted toward greatest need, decided quarterly
- Potential additions: CYO programs, parish youth ministries, Catholic school tuition aid

## Transparency
Post once per month: "Thanks to your support, we tithed $X this month to [specific cause]. Here's the receipt." This builds trust and attracts like-minded followers.

## 12-Month Revenue Projection (Conservative)
| Phase | Timeline | Followers | Monthly Revenue |
|-------|----------|-----------|-----------------|
| Launch | Month 1–3 | 500–2,000 | $0–200 (affiliates + lessons) |
| Growth | Month 4–6 | 5,000–10,000 | $500–1,500 |
| Momentum | Month 7–12 | 15,000–30,000 | $1,500–4,000+ |

## Monthly Tithe Target Path
- $200 revenue → $40 tithe (split 3 ways)
- $1,000 revenue → $200 tithe ($67 each)
- $2,500 revenue → $500 tithe ($167 each) ← **Milestone 1**
- $4,000 revenue → $800 tithe (weighted distribution)
